About DevConf 2026

DevConf is a non-profit technology conference established in 2018 with the goal of creating a strong connection between academic learning and real-world industry practices.

Over the years, DevConf has grown from a small community meetup into one of India’s most respected developer conferences, attracting participants from across the country.

DevConf 2026 continues this tradition by focusing on practical knowledge, strong technical foundations, and ethical innovation in technology.

The conference provides a platform for learning, collaboration, and career growth through talks, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Why Attend

  • Learn modern frameworks like React and Vue
  • Understand AI and Machine Learning fundamentals
  • Gain insights from experienced industry professionals
  • Build a strong professional network

Target Audience

  1. Undergraduate and graduate students
  2. Early-career software developers (0–3 years)
  3. Experienced engineers and tech leads
  4. Startup founders and tech entrepreneurs

Conference Themes

Web & Cloud Technologies

Modern web development, cloud platforms, and scalable architectures.

Artificial Intelligence & Data

Machine learning, data science, and intelligent systems.

DevOps & Security

Automation, CI/CD pipelines, and secure software practices.
